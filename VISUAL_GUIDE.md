## Example Use Cases

### Use Case 1: Blog Post
- **Title**: "10 Tips for Better Photography"
- **Featured Image**: Landscape photo
- **Excerpt**: "Improve your photography skills with these professional tips..."
- **Preview**: Shows exactly how it will look on Facebook, Twitter, etc.

### Use Case 2: Product Page (WooCommerce)
- **Title**: "Premium Wireless Headphones - $99.99"
- **Featured Image**: Product photo
- **Description**: "Experience crystal-clear audio with our premium headphones..."
- **Preview**: Shows how product will appear when shared

### Use Case 3: Landing Page (Elementor)
- **Title**: "Free eBook: Marketing Secrets"
- **Custom OG Image**: Ebook cover
- **Description**: "Download our comprehensive guide to digital marketing..."
- **Preview**: Preview directly in Elementor editor before publishing

## Tips for Best Previews

1. **Image Size**: Use 1200x630px for optimal display
2. **Title Length**: Keep under 60 characters
3. **Description**: Aim for 150-160 characters
4. **High Quality Images**: Use clear, high-resolution images
5. **Test Before Publishing**: Always check previews before going live
